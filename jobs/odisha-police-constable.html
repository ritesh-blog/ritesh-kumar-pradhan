Odisha Police Constable Recruitment 2025

The Odisha Police Department has released recruitment notification for Constable posts for 2025. Interested candidates can apply online before 30th July.

  • Application Start Date: 1st July 2025
  • Last Date to Apply: 30th July 2025
  • Vacancies: 1200
  • Eligibility: 10th Pass, Age 18-25 years
  • Official Website: https://odishapolice.gov.in

Please check the official notification for full details and documents required.
